<span data-ttu-id="73cb3-p101">Accès aux boîtes aux lettres, les données et les autres services Office 365, est contrôlé par l’utilisation des informations d’identification, telles qu’un nom d’utilisateur et mot de passe ou code confidentiel. With them the attacker can sign in as the original user and perform illicit actions. Using the stolen credentials, the attacker can access the user’s Office 365 mailbox, SharePoint folders, or files in the user's OneDrive. One action commonly seen is the attacker sending emails as the original user to recipients both inside and outside of the organization.
